About me

ZenLotusNosh - My name is Ninoshka. I am a Canadian citizen with Indian roots and Portuguese descent. I had been a competitive long distance swimmer in school and practiced yoga as part of my endurance training. I graduated as an Electrical Engineer and worked in the field for almost 10 years. During the covid pandemic, I took a break from work and decided to rediscover my passion...

I traveled to India to complete Yoga Teacher Training and deepen my Yoga practice. It was during this time that I truly fell in love with Yoga, learning the meaning of yoga, its philosophy, its meditative & healing benefits, surrounded by people sharing the same passion. It changed my outlook on life - Body, Mind, and Soul. I found purpose in sharing with others what I continuously learn in the daily practice of Yoga. Yoga means Unity - Unity of the Individual and Universal Consciousness.
